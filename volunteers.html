Chickasaw Center Fundraiser & Volunteers

The Chickasaw Senior Center holds a monthly fundraising event: an Indian taco sale. Customers travel from all over Duncan and the neighboring towns to enjoy the authentic tacos.

  • Location: Chickasaw Senior Center, Duncan, OK
  • Dates: Second Friday of the month
  • Hours: 10:30 a.m. - 1:30 p.m.
  • Volunteers
